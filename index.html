Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority.

Breakout takes your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting your data.

  • No Facial Data Collection: Our unique head tracking feature processes all facial movement data locally on your device in real-time. We do not collect, store, or transmit any facial information or camera data.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share your personal information with any entity.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics to track your behavior across other apps or websites.
  • Local Processing: All gameplay and tracking data is processed entirely on your device.
